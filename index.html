About A-1 Enterprises

Passion Turned Profession

A-1 Enterprises was founded from pure passion, dedication, and a lifelong connection with dogs. What began as a hobby during college days gradually transformed into a professional venture with a vision to elevate pet care and canine training standards in Meghalaya and across Northeast India.

From a small dream driven by love for dogs to becoming one of Meghalaya's growing canine service providers, A-1 Enterprises is committed to building a complete ecosystem for pets, training, grooming, boarding, rehabilitation, and future working dog development.

The founders personally experienced the challenges faced by pet parents, including housing limitations and the lack of professional canine facilities in the region. Instead of allowing those struggles to become obstacles, they became the motivation behind building something meaningful for the pet community.

Professionally established in 2020, A-1 Enterprises has steadily grown into a trusted name in dog training, grooming, rehabilitation, and pet services while continuously expanding its long-term vision of creating a complete pet ecosystem.

"We started as college students and passionate pet parents who were often thrown out of rental houses for having too many dogs. What began as an obsession and passion for dogs eventually turned into a profession, and later into a larger vision for the Northeast." — Founder, A-1 Enterprises

✧ Founder's Note

"A-1 Enterprises was built from passion, struggle, and dedication towards dogs. What started as a personal obsession gradually evolved into a profession, and today, into a larger vision of creating a complete ecosystem for pets and working dogs in the Northeast."

The journey has been strongly supported by family, especially by Mrs. Francisca S. Sangma, whose unwavering support and shared vision continue to play a vital role in the growth of A-1 Enterprises.

— Founder, A-1 Enterprises
